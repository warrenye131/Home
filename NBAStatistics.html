What are Statistics?

Just like every sport, the NBA also keeps track of their statistics as well. Such as points, rebounds, assists, steals, blocks, field goal percentage, field goal attempts, field goal mades, turnovers etc... These are especially important to coaches, stat guys, fantasy owners and even fans that want to brag about their favourite player being the best in the league.
