What is the NBA?

The NBA (National Basketball Association) and is home to 30 teams including one canadian team. (the Toronto Raptors) Home to the United States of America, the NBA has a net worth of around $634 million per team and is increasing every day. The NBA has attracted so much attention even teams that are from other leagues take every chance they can get to play an NBA team. The NBA is arguably home to the world's best basketball players with players such as Stephen Curry, Kevin Durant, Lebron James, Russell Westbrook and many more.

NBA Teams

There are a total of 30 teams in the NBA, ranging from Toronto to southern USA like Miami, Florida and Oakland/Golden State, California. Each team has their own unique play style and players (obviously), and thats because each and every team has different coaches and trainers with different general managers and owners, which means people with different opinions and different perspectives which leads to differences. That's why the NBA is so fun to watch, every team has a different play style so every game is different and unique to another.

Many teams throughout the years have become a so called dynasty and were very successful. They were so good they stick in the minds of fans as a potential argument for the greatest team of all time. Obviously this does not happen often, but when it does it is truly a special moment for the NBA and a great time to be a fan of that team or to watch that team even. But obviously when theres a good theres always a bad, so if the team you're rooting for isn't a great team or good team or even an ok team.
